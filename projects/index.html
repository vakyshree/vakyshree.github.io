FotoLounge

Fotolounge is a professional photography website. Collection of multi-genera photography art-work is showcased here. The company provides wide range of services like wedding photography, event photography, fashion photography, also pictures involving travel, culture, lifestyle and much more.

Case Study :

To embrace the collection of variant colours and multi- genera of pictures, dark background was chosen. Black background led the photos to show in contrast. Modern design techniques are integrated with CMS (content management system) to manage presentation and content. Optimized images made the processing time very low and the website was highly efficient and lightweight.

College Korner

College Korner is a E-learning platform where students and professors interact, share and post. It's a place where people solve each others problems and make discussions on variety of topics. Profile analysed is there to make students understand their strengths and weaknesses.

Case Study :

The approach in this project is to ease the student and professor's interaction outside the classroom and to promote various activity-clubs in college. Professors can issue notices to inform particular students, integrating this feature with security factors. Students performances in various practice tests is analysed by out profile analysed to give them detailed report. Reward system is added to appreciate the contributions of each individual.
